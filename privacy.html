Privacy Policy

Application: Studio Scheduler

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Studio Scheduler is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use this application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Studio Scheduler, we may collect:

  • Your Google account email address.
  • Your basic profile information provided by Google Sign-In.
  • Google Calendar authorization granted by you.
  • Studio booking information required to schedule recording sessions.

We never collect or store your Google account password.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Authenticate users using Google Sign-In.
  • Create studio booking events in your Google Calendar.
  • Update or remove booking events when schedules change.
  • Provide booking notifications and reminders.
  • Maintain proper scheduling of studio resources.

3. Google Calendar Access

Studio Scheduler requests access to your Google Calendar only after you explicitly grant permission.

Google Calendar data is used solely for creating, updating, and deleting calendar events related to studio bookings.

We do not read, analyze, sell, or use your calendar information for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Information may only be disclosed:

  • When required by applicable law.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • With your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Data Retention

Only the information necessary to operate Studio Scheduler is retained. Google OAuth credentials are stored securely and used solely for authorized calendar operations.

7. Revoking Access

You may revoke Studio Scheduler's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account security settings and removing the application's access.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
